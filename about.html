About Bowen Executive

Specialist retained executive search in U.S. civil construction.

Nick Prokop - Founder

Nick grew up in a civil construction family. His parents were both civil engineers who moved overseas, built a life, and grew their own construction business from the ground up — his father in structural steel fabrication, his mother running her own business in formwork, loading platforms, and safety screens.

Construction was not something explained to Nick. It was just how things were. Drawings on the table, hard hats at the door, conversations about sites, schedules, and projects. Major infrastructure going up across Sydney's CBD was the backdrop of everyday life.

That upbringing shaped everything that followed.

Nick has been in recruitment since 2012. Over that time his focus narrowed deliberately — toward construction, then civil construction specifically, then the U.S. market. Six years ago he moved to North America and committed fully to the U.S. civil sector.

That is not a pivot. It is a progression that started long before recruitment did.

What that means for you

Civil construction professionals are busy. Projects don't wait and schedules don't move. The last thing a GC owner or a Superintendent needs is a recruiter who has to be walked through the basics.

When you work with Bowen Executive, you are talking to someone who already understands the work — the pressures of a heavy civil job, what it takes to run a wet utilities programme, and what good field leadership actually looks like on a civil project. That context does not have to be explained. It is already there.

Texas is our primary market

One of the most active heavy civil and infrastructure markets in the country. We recruit throughout the United States with a strong concentration in Texas and surrounding states.

Heavy civil. Wet utilities. Civil infrastructure. Nothing outside it.
