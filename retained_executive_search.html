The cost of getting it wrong

  • Replacing a senior leader costs between 150–213% of their annual salary

  • Over 30% of U.S. construction executives reach retirement age by 2028

  • Project delays, team disruption, and lost institutional knowledge are not in that number

The cost of getting a senior search wrong has never been higher.

Why Retained Search

  • Retained search carries a 90% placement success rate versus 25% for contingency

  • Fully dedicated search — one firm, one focus, one outcome

  • A thorough vetting process that goes beyond the resume — track record, culture fit, longevity

  • Access to candidates who are not actively looking and would not respond to a job posting
