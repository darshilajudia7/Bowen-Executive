Leadership Continuity & Succession

The civil construction industry is facing a leadership transition it is not prepared for.

  • The average U.S. construction executive is 58 years old
  • 45% of leadership positions are held by individuals over 55
  • 41% of the construction workforce is projected to retire by 2031
  • Only 35% of U.S. construction firms have a formal succession plan
  • Companies with a documented succession plan trade at a 40% premium to industry average

Most GCs know the problem exists. Few have done anything about it.

What we do

We work with GC owners and senior leadership teams thinking beyond the next hire. Not recruitment — a strategic conversation about who runs the business in five years, and what needs to happen between now and then.

Structural Engineering

Designing robust foundations and load-bearing frameworks for commercial and residential developments.

Project Management

Overseeing construction timelines, resource allocation, and site safety from groundbreaking to completion.

Sustainable Infrastructure

Integrating eco-friendly materials and energy-efficient designs into modern urban environments.

Who this is for

GC owners and senior leaders who have built something worth protecting — and want the right people in place to continue it.
